About the Author

Aditya Mohan, a rising name in fitness industry, is a dedicated individual who believes in following his passion.
His passion for fitness keeps him motivated physically, mentally and spiritually.
With more than a decade of experience in the fitness line, Aditya has observed the industry very closely.
Trained by Dronacharya awardee Sh. Bhupender Dhawan, one of the most renowned names of fitness industry in India,
Aditya currently runs his own gym by the name of ‘Fitness Tree’.
With his experience he has come to a conclusion that if one has to sustain his fitness for long, it is only possible by following the mantra - “Be a hard worker, a consistent learner, a perseverant self-trainer and a passionate go-getter.’’

With the same perception, he has brought out a range of e-books under the name,
‘Adifit Training Program’.
